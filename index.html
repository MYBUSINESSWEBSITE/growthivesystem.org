Meet the Founder & CEO

David Ayogu

"Helping businesses move from scattered efforts to structured, scalable, high-performing systems."

Growthive Systems was founded by David Ayogu, a psychology graduate and creative strategist driven by a vision to help small and medium businesses grow beyond just visibility. With a background in Industrial-Organizational Psychology, graphic design, video editing, human resource analytics, and business operations, he combines creativity, strategy, and people-focused systems to build brands that are not only seen but also organized for long-term success.

His approach is rooted in clarity, discipline, and purpose — integrating digital presence, business strategy, and human structure into one unified system. Beyond business, his foundation in faith and leadership continues to shape a mission centered on growth, value creation, and meaningful impact.
